Privacy Policy

Effective June 25, 2026 · Applies to the gj-digital.com website, operated by GJ Digital LLC.

The short version: this site collects nothing about you. gj-digital.com is a simple, static website with no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. The only personal information we ever receive is what you choose to send us by email.

This website

gj-digital.com is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We set no cookies and run no analytics or third-party tracking scripts. As with any website, the hosting provider (GitHub) may log standard web-server request data — such as IP addresses and browser type — to deliver the site and protect against abuse, as described in GitHub's privacy statement. We do not have access to a personally identifiable visitor log.

When you contact us

If you email info@gj-digital.com — for example, using a contact link on this site — we receive your email address and whatever you include in your message. We use that information only to respond to you and to discuss the work you're inquiring about. We don't sell it, and we don't add you to any marketing list.

Our products and client work

GJ Digital builds software for clients and develops its own apps. How data is handled inside any specific application or client engagement is governed by that product's or project's own agreement and privacy policy — not by this one. For example, our app GiVo Stream has its own Privacy Policy. This policy covers only this website.

What we don't do

  • No user accounts or sign-ups on this site.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or tracking pixels.
  • No advertising and no third-party trackers.
  • No sale or sharing of personal information.

Children

This site is not directed at children and collects no information from anyone, children included.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.
